Please cosponsor H.R. 938, the Nuclear Family Priority Act. 

It will eliminate the Chain Migration that began in the late 1950s, and it will return annual immigration to lower and more sensible levels. It will speed up the immigration of spouses and minor children but break the chain of immigration of extended family. 

The United States already has nearly 50 million foreign workers and their dependents, with a million more coming in a year (not even counting illegal aliens). We need less future immigration. 

Chain Migration is the primary mechanism that has caused annual legal immigration in this country to quadruple from the 1960s. It serves no national interest or humanitarian purpose. And it is a huge magnet for illegal immigration, as well. 

Cosponsoring H.R. 938 may be the single, most effective thing you can do to reduce the pressure of immigration on our communities.

Phone me if you would like to talk about this,

Mr. Greg Raven

P.S. We must preserve America for Americans.
