As one of your constituents and a proud member of Gun Owners of America, I strongly urge you to support the Safeguard American Voter Eligibility (SAVE) Act and oppose any attempt to weaken or block it.

Gun owners have watched anti‑Second Amendment politicians manipulate election rules to entrench their power and attack our rights. The SAVE America Act (S. 128) is a common‑sense measure to restore trust in our elections by making sure only American citizens decide who governs us.

The bill's core ideas are straightforward and widely supported: require proof of U.S. citizenship to register for federal elections, keep voter rolls limited to eligible voters, and put real teeth into laws against illegal voting. Without secure elections, every constitutional protection --- including the Second Amendment --- is at risk.

Gun owners like myself are watching. We will remember who stood for secure elections and our constitutional rights.

[Greg Raven](https://www.gregraven.online/), Apple Valley, CA
