The White House and Senate Republicans have reached a new low with their latest taxpayer-subsidized illegal alien amnesty bill!

Twenty-two thousand dollars a year. That is what each foreign worker household with a high school drop out costs American workers in taxes, according to a recent study soon to be released by the Heritage Foundation. The costs of these households on the American taxpayer total more than $100 billion a year, making our current immigration system the largest unfunded mandate on local and state governments. 

Why would our elected officials want to import foreign workers who require such huge infusions of taxpayer cash? 

And why would they propose increasing that flow? 

Even more astounding is that they want to force American taxpayers to continue to subsidize those who have come here illegally or broken our laws to stay. 

Why would the Senate and House Democratic leadership --- and now the White House and most Senate Republicans --- want to keep these lawbreakers --- every one of whose households will cost Americans an average net of $22,000 a year? 

American taxpayers need a legion of heroes to protect us from these outrageous subsidies. Whose side are you on?
