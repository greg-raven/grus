Please support and pass H.R. 2 when it reaches the House floor.

The Supreme Court just confirmed that migrants who stop on the far side of our border are not automatically entitled to claim asylum here --- simply by reading the statute Congress wrote.

Now it is Congress's turn to finish the job. When H.R. 2, the Secure the Border Act, comes to the floor for the vote you have been promised, I am asking you to be there and pass it. H.R. 2 would make these protections permanent --- tightening asylum standards, mandating E-Verify, criminalizing visa overstays, and reforming the parole authority that has been so badly abused.

A secure border should not depend on who occupies the White House. Please vote to pass H.R. 2 and help build an immigration system that works for the American people.
